About Me

Background

My background is in linguistics as both a Ph.D. student and as a Teaching Assistant.

What is linguistics? It is the study of languages including:

  • Language Structure (grammar system, phonology/phonetics, semantics, etc.)
  • Language and its use in society
  • Language documentation and revitalization

Important! When I mention that I am a linguist, I have often been asked one of two questions:

  1. How many languages do you speak?
  2. Am I speaking correctly? Will you be correcting my grammar?

  • First, while I do speak a couple languages, I am mainly concerend in language patterns.

  • Second, linguistics is not concerned with prescriptive grammar. It is concerened with descriptive grammar; that is, how language is used. There is no wrong way to speak a language, nor is there a right way to speak.

My research has focused on bilingualism, second language acquisition, and language change, but language as a whole is absolutely fascinating!
